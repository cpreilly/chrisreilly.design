My Skillset

I am fluent in all aspects of design, starting out in print and learning typography and layout, then jumping into the digital world with design and front end coding (html,css). I am empathetic to users needs and create experiences for them to drive conversions and results via:

Discovery

Competitive analysis, user research as well as analytical measurements are combed through to find weakness and strengths to any project. Stakeholder kickoff meetings are arranged to get the team on the same page and figure out what our objectives are.

Architecture

Information architecture is the base to any project, sitemaps, user-flows as well as user personas are created to understand how the project will work and for user centric design to take shape. Red routes and major interaction are established to get users to their main objectes as quickly and easily as possible.

Prototyping

From quick sketches, and wireframing to high fidelity prototypes I use user and stakeholder feedback to create experiences that serve a purpose while remaining empathetic to the user. After approval of mockups annotated versions are created and delivered to development.

Validation

User testing is where I learn if the product works, using software and/or in-person interviews I can validate externally and internally if our changes are working. Also analytics and other metrics are poured over to find hints of success or failure, where we can pivot and make changes.

Iteration

Design is never finished, we always learn something new from our users and research. Egos must be left at the door so that we can retain a cyclic process of prototyping, testing, analyzing, and refining a product or process. Based on the results of testing the most recent iteration of a design, changes and refinements are made.

Support

I also can create animations for devs to better get a grasp of what we are after. I also have experience working on version control systems and can jump in where needed on frontend tasks. With a history in graphic design I can also help with branding, marketing collaterals, SEO optimization and any other design needed to help products reach their full potential.
